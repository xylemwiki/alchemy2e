Alchemy 2nd Edition

Alchemy 2nd Edition was discontinued in 2024 and was originally going to feature a complete from the ground reworking of Alchemy. Alchemy 2nd Edition did not plan to have Potions and removed Possession, Scrying Pool, and Philosipher's Stone. This set was worked on entierly by Weston S. and had no involvment from other Xylem memebers. The expansion icon is .

Throughout its development only 2 Kingdom cards were every created and introduced the Ingredient marker and 1 Recipe named Growth. Recipie cards followed the color scheme [1.2, 0.9, 1.35], and they were triggered by using different ingredient cards. If you had the required cards to trigger a recipie you may choose to discard those cards and follow the effect of the Recipie.

Potion costs, depending on the value of the cards, could have converted into or additional coins to the cost. There were a planned 30 Kingdom cards in total for the set. The game design was not enticing and no effect for cards such as Growth were ever made.
